Project Dr. One: A Decisive Response to Aerial Threats

The Strategic Imperative

The Kurdistan Region faces a sustained and calculated campaign of hostile UAS attacks designed to undermine national security and economic stability. This is not a random threat, but a form of economic warfare targeting the heart of the region's prosperity. Conventional defenses are proving ineffective, creating a critical security gap that demands an immediate and sovereign solution.

The Risks of Inaction

Cascading Economic Damage

Continued attacks on energy infrastructure risk billions in lost revenue, deter foreign investment, and threaten funding for essential public services.

Erosion of Sovereignty

Failure to control the region's airspace cedes a strategic advantage to adversaries and undermines the KRG's authority and international standing.

The Dr. One Engagement Sequence

Project Dr. One is a layered, integrated defense built on the military-standard D-TID-N kill chain. Each step is a link in a rapid and robust response cycle, illustrated by the continuously animated sequence below.

1. DETECT

IRIS radar provides 24/7, 360° airspace surveillance.

2. TRACK

A stable, high-fidelity 3D track is established.

3. CLASSIFY

Micro-Doppler & AI confirm the threat type.

4. CUE

Target data is sent to the DroneCatcher GCS.

5. LAUNCH

DroneCatcher autonomously launches to intercept.

6. ENGAGE

Onboard sensors lock on and fire the net gun.

7. NEUTRALIZE

The threat is captured for safe recovery and analysis.
